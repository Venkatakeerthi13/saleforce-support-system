Stakeholder Mapping Description
Stakeholder mapping identifies the key individuals and groups involved in the development, implementation, and usage of the Salesforce AI-Powered Customer Support Ticketing System. Each stakeholder has specific roles, responsibilities, and interests in ensuring the successful delivery and operation of the system.

The primary stakeholders include customers who raise support requests, support agents who handle and resolve cases, support managers who monitor performance and SLA compliance, and system administrators responsible for configuring, maintaining, and securing the Salesforce application. Business stakeholders provide requirements and validate that the system aligns with organizational goals.

Effective stakeholder mapping ensures clear communication, proper role-based access, and alignment between business needs and technical implementation throughout the project lifecycle.
